## Biography

KAF is a Japanese virtual singer who debuted on October 18, 2018, as the very first artist under KAMITSUBAKI STUDIO. She is known for her ethereal, emotionally resonant singing voice and distinct musical style.

Despite being a virtual existence, she has built deep connections with fans in the real world through her music and cross-media projects.

## History

- **2018**: Debuted on YouTube on October 18.
- **2019**: Held her first "Fukakai" solo live concert.
- **2021**: Formed the virtual singer group V.W.P with RIM, Harusaruhi, Isekaijoucho, and KOKO.
- **2024**: Held the massive "Kaika" live concert at Yoyogi National Stadium.

## Musical Style

Her music is primarily produced by Kanzaki Iori. The songs often explore themes of adolescent confusion, urban loneliness, and the boundary between virtuality and reality.
